Bartek Pierściński (Kraków, 2001)

Bartek is an enigma in the graphic design world. He works with coding and visual narrative, though no one knows where he learnt these skills. His work often takes the form of intricate, thought-provoking art, leaving viewers with an unsettled feeling and questions they can't answer. Bartek remains silent on his background and past, so the mystery of his identity remains.
